The History of AChitThit Cafe

AChitThit Café, located in Yankin, Yangon, has been a beloved local spot since its founding
in 2008 by Lilium Ruby Chan.The name “AChitThit,” meaning “new love” in Burmese, reflects the
founder’s passion for creating a fresh and meaningful café experience.

The café is well-known for using authentic coffee beans from Shan State’s Taunggyi region,
ensuring a rich and aromatic flavor. It also uses pure milk from an organic farm, giving
customers a healthier, natural taste in every cup.

AChitThit Café offers a quiet and comfortable environment, perfect for students and workers.
To support them, the café provides discounts to those with student or work IDs. It also has private
rooms available for important meetings, making it a versatile space for both relaxation and business.

Additionally, customers can enjoy free Wi-Fi and complimentary jasmine leaf tea, adding to the welcoming
atmosphere.Over the years, AChitThit has become more than just a coffee shop—it’s a community hub where
people come to study, work, meet, and create. It remains a symbol of dedication, local pride,
and warm hospitality in Yangon.
